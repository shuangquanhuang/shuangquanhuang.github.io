Online Stock Span

问题

Write a class StockSpanner which collects daily price quotes for some stock, and returns the span of that stock’s price for the current day.

The span of the stock’s price today is defined as the maximum number of consecutive days (starting from today and going backwards) for which the price of the stock was less than or equal to today’s price.

For example, if the price of a stock over the next 7 days were $[100, 80, 60, 70, 60, 75, 85]$, then the stock spans would be $[1, 1, 1, 2, 1, 4, 6]$.

就是找数组中，当前数字数字结尾的最大子区间的最大值是当前数字。

例子

Node

  1. Calls to StockSpanner.next(int price) will have 1 <= price <= 10^5.
  2. There will be at most 10000 calls to StockSpanner.next per test case.
  3. There will be at most 150000 calls to StockSpanner.next across all test cases.
  4. The total time limit for this problem has been reduced by 75% for C++, and 50% for all other languages.

分析

暴力法是遍历每个元素，然后向前搜索第一个比他大的元素，复杂度$O(N^2)$。
主要要考虑的是如何加速搜索比他大的元素的过程。

  1. 如果前面一个元素比当前元素大，那么子区间长度是$1$，停止查找。
  2. 如果前面一个元素比当前元素小，那么可能比当前大的元素一定是比前面一个元素大。

那么假设有个函数$f(i)$是第一个比$A[i]$大的元素的位置，即$A[f(i)] > A[i]$，就能递归的完成上面的步骤2。

所以没有好的方法的时候，我们可以考虑如何优化暴力法。
